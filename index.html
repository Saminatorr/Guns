Firework

It all started with the chinese. They were the first to invent gunpowder which is used to fire a gun. However, they didn't start with a gun we see today. They started out with a simple barrel of bamboo which was used for fireworks. But the bamboo was not strong enough and they made a metal barrel together with projectiles that fit perfectly. And without knowing, they made a gun. All it takes is gunpowder, a barrel and a projectile.
